Super Mario Bros.

Super Mario Bros. is a classic platformer game developed and published by Nintendo. It was released in 1985 for the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) and has since become one of the most iconic video games of all time. The game follows Mario, a plumber, as he journeys through the Mushroom Kingdom to rescue Princess Toadstool from the evil Bowser.
